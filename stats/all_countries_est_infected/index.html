Estimated Number of Infections: By Country

The following table contains a ranking of countries based on the estimated number of infections within the country. The estimation is based on the assumption that the disease causes a death rate of 1.38% (see also Verity R, Okell LC, Dorigatti I, Winskill P, Whittaker C, Imai N, et al. Estimates of the severity of coronavirus disease 2019: a model-based analysis. Lancet Infect Dis 2020). Given that there are usually 14 days between confirming COVID-19 case and potential death, these numbers represent the total estimates of confirmed cases 14 days ago (see also Vollmer and Bommer, University of Goettingen).
