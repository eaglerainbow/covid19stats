The doubling time below are calculated based on

  • an exponential growth assumption
  • for time difference of past seven (7) days. The doubling time’s unit is “days”.

The first doubling time indicates the increase of confirmed (infected) cases. There, the higher the number is, the better is to take control of the disease.

The second doubling time indicates the increase of recovered (healed) cases. There, the lower the number is, the better it is to take control of the disease.
